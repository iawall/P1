Toby Keith: Legendary 'Courtesy of the Red, White and Blue' singer dead at 62

Country music star Toby Keith passed away at 62 after battling stomach cancer.
Known for hits like "Courtesy of the Red, White & Blue," Keith sold over 40 million albums and was a strong advocate for American military service members, performing on 11 USO tours.
Fans and friends mourn his loss..
